Deep Photo Style Transfer

'Deep Photo Style Transfer' further developed Leon A. Gatys' paper, "A Neural Algorithm of Artistic Style," from the perspective of photography, not painting.

By using content & style loss funcion from Luan's paper, there's crushing of detailed content and spillover phenomena.

To deal with those problems, this paper applied 'Photorealism Regularization' and 'Augmented Style Loss with Semantic Segmentation'
